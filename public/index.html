Welcome to MindMirror — an amazing interactive mental health self-assessment platform!

Get ready to take control of your emotional well-being and psychological state — that is exactly what this excellent project is about! Completing a scientifically-developed survey is an engaging step on your way to better mental health. Completing it, you will receive a personalized report on your mental health state, including stress, depression, anxiety, social adaptation, and others.

MindMirror is designed for students and adults who want to try out their mental health.
You can learn about mental health conditions, view your results against average, and get suggestions on how to improve your well-being.

All responses are anonymous and confidential, so you can be sure your identity is kept safe and secure.
It is not a diagnosis but an excellent first step in becoming aware of oneself and seeking assistance if needed.
